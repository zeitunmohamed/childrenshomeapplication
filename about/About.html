BACKGROUND INFORMATION

Najah Childrenâ€™s Home is an orphanage with humble inception that was registered by the Registrar General of the Societies SOC/1984, Registration No 11520 of 18th January 1986. The founding fathers were moved by the increasing number of needy Muslim orphans that were languishing in poverty with no care and no hope as the area had only one orphanage and could not cater for all the orphans. They pulled their resources together and rent some few rooms in a residential premises in Garissa town and admitted the first batch of 60 orphans. The founders were well- visioned and acquired land in 1989 for the institution in the Iftin Location of Garissa County for they were quite optimistic that someday the institution would grow.

By the grace of Allah the orphanage got good will support for the construction of six rooms and a dining hall from the organization of Islamic Heritage & Culture.

Mission

To empower children by providing a safe environment, access to education, and the tools they need to thrive and succeed.

Later in 1993 (1412 A.H) a dormitory with a capacity of 145 orphans was built and the same number of orphans sponsored. A mosque was also built to cater for 250 persons.

Vission

To create a world where every child has the opportunity to lead a happy, healthy, and fulfilling life.
